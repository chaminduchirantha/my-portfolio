where HTML defines the basic framework,CSS handles the visual styling, and JavaScript adds interactivity.

UI/UX Design

UI/UX design, paragraphs play a crucial role in structuring content and enhancing readability.
According to the Stanford UX Guide, paragraphs on the web should be much shorter than in print to accommodate the way users scan content quickly.
